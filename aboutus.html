ABOUT US

The legacy of Furniture Arts

Way back in the mid 60s, Mr.Dayabhai Mistry honed his skills in the family business of carpentry. Talent, Dedication, Hard work and Persistence transformed him into an accomplished entrepreneur. As the business grew, knowledge and business values were passed on to his sons Harisiddh, Sudhir & Hulash. It was just a matter of time before the family business scaled up and in 1972 transformed into Furniture Arts. Today we have set a benchmark for Quality, dedication and project completion within Deadlines.
